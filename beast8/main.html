Organize your todo list by nesting your todos

How to use:

  1. (add a todo), ---------------------- type something in the input and click add.
  2. (edit todo), ----------------------- double clicked on a todo and when done typing click enter key.
  3. (delete todo), -------------------- click delete button.
  4. (add a todo inside a todo), ----- click the add button next to the todo then type something then click the enter key.
  5. (completed a todo), ------------- click completed button.
